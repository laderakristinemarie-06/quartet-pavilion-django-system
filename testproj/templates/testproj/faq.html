Booking

You can send us an inquiry through our booking form, or reach us directly via our Facebook page or email. Once we confirm availability, we'll send you a reservation agreement and payment instructions to secure your date.

For weddings and large events, we recommend booking at least 6–12 months in advance, especially for peak season dates (December–February). For intimate gatherings and smaller events, 2–3 months lead time is generally sufficient — though earlier is always better.

Yes. A non-refundable reservation fee is required to officially hold your chosen date. The remaining balance is settled in agreed installments leading up to your event. Full payment details are outlined in your reservation agreement.

Absolutely — we welcome and encourage site visits. Contact us to schedule a walkthrough with one of our event coordinators, who will personally show you the venues and answer any questions you have on-site.

Venue & Capacity

Capacity varies by venue and setup style:

  • Birthday & Private Venue — up to 150 guests
  • Wedding & Romantic Venue — up to 200 guests
  • Family Milestones Venue — up to 250 guests
  • Academic & Youth Venue — up to 180 guests
  • Corporate & Formal Venue — up to 200 guests
  • Entertainment & Special Venue — up to 300 guests

Exact capacities depend on your chosen layout (banquet, cocktail, theatre, etc.).

Quartet Pavilion features a mix of lush open gardens and covered indoor spaces spread across our 4-hectare property. Most event packages can be tailored to use either or both, giving you flexibility depending on the occasion and weather.

Yes. All outdoor venue bookings include access to covered backup spaces. Our team monitors weather leading up to your event and will coordinate with you in advance if an indoor configuration is recommended.

Basic venue setup — tables, chairs, linens, and general lighting — is included in the venue package. Florals, themed décor, and specialty lighting arrangements can be arranged through our in-house team or approved partner suppliers for an additional fee.

Yes, we have ample on-site parking for guests. For very large events, our team can coordinate additional parking arrangements and shuttle service if needed. Please discuss your guest count with us at the time of booking.

Catering

Yes. We offer in-house catering with a range of menu packages suited to different event types and budgets. Our menus feature a mix of Filipino and international cuisine, and can be customised to your preferences and dietary requirements.

Outside catering is allowed on a case-by-case basis and may be subject to a corkage or coordination fee. All outside caterers must be pre-approved by our team. Please discuss this with us early in the planning process.

Yes. Our kitchen team can accommodate common dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-specific requests. Please inform us at least two weeks before your event so we can prepare accordingly.

Food tastings are available for qualified bookings (typically weddings and large corporate events). Please coordinate with your assigned event coordinator to schedule a tasting session once your package is confirmed.

Accommodation

Yes. We offer Superior and Deluxe rooms designed for a restful, Provence-inspired staycation experience. Rooms can be booked independently or bundled with an event package. Visit our Rooms page to explore options.

Absolutely. Many couples and hosts choose to stay overnight before or after their event. Room availability is subject to your event date, so we recommend reserving accommodation at the same time as your venue.

Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged subject to room availability and may incur an additional fee. Please let us know your preferred schedule when booking your room.

Policies

Reservation fees are non-refundable. If you need to reschedule, we allow one complimentary date change provided at least 60 days' notice is given and the new date is available. Cancellations within 30 days of the event are subject to the full balance being owed. Full terms are detailed in your reservation agreement.

Standard event time slots run from morning to midnight. Events are expected to wrap up by 12:00 AM to respect our neighbors and permit conditions. Extended hours may be available for select venues upon request.

Well-behaved pets on leash are welcome in outdoor areas of the property. Pets are not permitted inside indoor venues or accommodation rooms. Please inform us in advance if you plan to bring a pet to your event.

Alcohol is permitted at events. In-house bar packages are available for an additional fee. Outside alcohol is allowed with a corkage fee applied per bottle. Responsible service of alcohol is observed; we reserve the right to stop service if necessary.

Yes — Quartet Pavilion is a popular location for prenup shoots, music videos, content creation, and editorial photography. Shoot packages are available separately from event bookings. Contact us to check availability and rates.
