Who We Are

Quartet Pavilion is a 4-hectare Provence-inspired farm retreat nestled in the heart of nature — a sanctuary designed for those who believe that the most beautiful things in life always take time.

We are a destination for rest, creativity, and celebration. Whether you are seeking a quiet escape from the noise of the city, planning an intimate wedding, or looking for a dream backdrop for your prenuptial shoot, Quartet Pavilion was built with you in mind.

Every stone path, every flower carefully chosen, every brushstroke on our custom headboard paintings — it all whispers the same quiet truth: slow down, be present, this moment is worth savoring.

FILM & PRODUCTION
your next shooting location

Quartet Pavilion is not just a place to rest — it is a world-class production location for artists, directors, and production teams. With 4 hectares of diverse, stunning scenery all within one property, you get multiple distinct looks and settings without ever leaving the grounds. From lush garden paths to rustic farm structures, Provence-style architecture, open fields, and intimate indoor spaces — every corner tells a different story, perfect for TV commercials, music videos, indie films, magazine shoots, and celebrity features.

OUR JOURNEY
how it all began
THE BEGINNING

THE DREAM IS PLANTED

Quartet Pavilion was born from a simple but powerful idea — to create a space that goes against the rush of modern life. The founders envisioned a farm that restores, inspires, and celebrates.

THE BUILDING

THE GROUNDS TAKE SHAPE

Across 4 hectares, every corner of Quartet Pavilion was carefully built by hand — from the garden paths to the hand-painted headboards in each room, inspired by the beauty of Provence.

FIRST GUESTS

DOORS OPEN TO THE WORLD

Quartet Pavilion welcomed its first guests — couples, families, artists, and dreamers — who immediately fell in love with the pace and the beauty the farm had to offer.

GOING VIRAL

THE WORD SPREADS

Word of Quartet Pavilion spread organically through social media as guests shared their stunning photos and heartfelt experiences — drawing couples, influencers, and media from across the Philippines.

TODAY & BEYOND

STILL GROWING, STILL BLOOMING

Quartet Pavilion continues to grow — adding new experiences, refining every detail, and welcoming thousands of guests who come seeking rest, creativity, and celebration.
